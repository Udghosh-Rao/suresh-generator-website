FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our generator services

What generator capacity do I need for my home? +

For residential use, calculate your total load: 1-2 BHK needs 5-10 kVA, 3 BHK needs 10-15 kVA, and larger homes or societies need 15-25 kVA. We provide free load assessment to determine the exact capacity required for your specific needs.

Do you provide installation services? +

Yes! We provide complete turnkey installation including site preparation, electrical connections, ATS panel setup, fuel line installation, and commissioning. Our certified technicians ensure safe and compliant installation as per industry standards.

What is included in your AMC service? +

Our AMC includes periodic inspections, oil & filter changes, battery maintenance, cooling system check, fuel system cleaning, load testing, and priority breakdown support. We offer quarterly, half-yearly, and annual AMC plans tailored to your usage.

Is 24/7 emergency service really available? +

Absolutely! We maintain a dedicated emergency response team available round-the-clock. Call +91 9829325775 anytime for breakdown support. Our technicians typically reach within 2-4 hours depending on your location.

Do you offer generator rental services? +

Yes, we offer flexible rental options for events, construction sites, emergency backup, and temporary power needs. Rental includes delivery, installation, operator (if needed), fuel management, and maintenance during the rental period.

Which generator brands do you service? +

We service all major brands including Cummins, Kirloskar, Mahindra, Ashok Leyland, Caterpillar, Perkins, Volvo Penta, and KOEL. Our technicians are trained and experienced with both Indian and international generator makes.
